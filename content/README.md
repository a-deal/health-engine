# Content Strategy

Post drafts and distribution planning for health-engine.

## Voice

Just a guy trying to understand his health. Not a health influencer. Not selling anything. Engineer who did the research and is sharing what he found. Data carries the weight.

- Short sentences. The period is a hammer.
- Lead with the number, punch with the implication.
- Real data from real draws. Never embellish.
- Acknowledge uncertainty. "I don't know yet" is honest.
- No emojis. No hype words. No "game-changer" or "excited to announce."
- No em dashes. No heavy quotation marks.
- CTA deferred to the end, low pressure.

## Channels

- **LinkedIn** — primary. Andrew's professional network, health-adjacent audience.
- **X** — secondary. Threads for deeper dives (10 metrics, cost comparison, sleep regularity).
- **Reddit** — targeted. r/bloodwork, r/quantifiedself, r/biohackers for credibility posts.
