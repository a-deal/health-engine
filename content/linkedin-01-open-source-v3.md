# LinkedIn Post #1 (v3): The AI Health Coach That Knows Your Numbers

**Status:** Draft — needs Andrew review
**Platform:** LinkedIn
**Angle:** Agent story with Paul partnership. What's happening, who's building it, why it matters.

---

I built an AI health coach that texts me every morning.

It pulls my sleep data, heart rate, weight trend, and lab history. It knows what I've measured, what I haven't, and what to do next. It coaches me on one thing at a time based on my actual numbers.

Last week I let it coach someone else. He sent 60+ biomarkers, 3 lab draws, workout logs, and his full supplement stack. The first thing the system flagged wasn't a lab value. It was that he was taking 8 supplements without confirming his sleep foundation was solid.

That's the failure mode of health optimization culture. Skip the boring stuff, jump to the shiny stuff. The system caught it because it checks foundations first: sleep, movement, nutrition, recovery. If those aren't locked in, nothing else matters yet.

I'm building this with Paul Mederos, who's spent a decade in behavioral health and consumer wellness (Kasane, Enchant Health, Territory Foods). He's shaping the coaching layer — how the system meets people where they are and builds trust over time.

It runs on a Mac Mini in my apartment. Your data never leaves your machine. The scoring engine is open source: 20 metrics, NHANES percentiles, 121 tests, MIT license.

Yesterday I texted my dad from it. He doesn't know what MCP is. He doesn't need to. He got a message from a coach who's about to learn his health picture and help him build on it.

We're onboarding a small group right now. Link in comments.
